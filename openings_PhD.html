Openings for Ph.D. Students

Our team currently has multiple openings for Ph.D. students in the area of analog and mixed-signal integrated circuits (IC). Our research themes are centered on:
1. High-speed, high precision data converters for wireless and wireline communications
2. IC design for neural interfacing and neuromodulation
3. IC design for accelerating machine learning

We are particularly looking for candidates who have/are:
1. Solid background in circuit analysis
2. Experience in analog and mixed-signal IC design and tape out
3. Proficient in using IC design, simulation, and layout tools (e.g., Cadence, Synopsys, etc.)
4. Enthusiastic about IC design, good communication and teamworking skills

If you are interested in applying for these positions, please email the following materials to Prof. Xilin Liu at xilinliu at ece dot utoronto dot ca:
1. A cover letter stating the research interest area (1-3) and relevant experience
2. Latest CV
3. (Unofficial) transcripts of undergraduate and master’s studies
4. Sample of publications (if applicable)

Successful candidates will receive full financial support that covers tuition, stipend, insurance, etc. Review of applications will begin immediately and continue until the positions are filled.

Statement on Equity, diversity and inclusion (EDI):
Evidence clearly shows that increasing equity, diversity and inclusion (EDI) in research environments enhances excellence, innovation and creativity. I am committed to promote EDI in my research team and student training environment. I strongly encourage people with diverse backgrounds, especially those from underrepresented groups, to join my team.
